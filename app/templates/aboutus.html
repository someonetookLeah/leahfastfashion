About Us!

Hello this is the 'NALI Project' and our Senior Capstone for the Computer Science Academy 2022-2023. Our project is about fast fashion so we created this website so that you can swap or giveaway clothing that you no longer want so that you can help advocate toward ending fast fashion. We all have differnt experiences with fashion and not everyone is aware of what it is. Fast fashion is very cheap and highly accessible, but we hope that with our website, you can help reduce your fast fashion footprint to help the earth, people, and economy! Below you can read about what fast fashion is and below that you can learn about the members individually!

What is Fast Fashion?


Fast fashion is clothing that is made with cheap materials and cheap labor. It's clothing that is made to be cheap, follow the current trends, and is made to meet consumer demand. It's severely unethical and not environmentally friendly in any way. The industry produces high volumes of clothing, which means that the clothing the consumers pay for is made with poor quality. The industry also employs workers from third world countries with low wages, exploiting them and exposing them to dangerous work conditions. The environmental impact on fast fashions consists of its use of non-renewable items, greenhouse gases, and its massive use of water and energy to create clothing.
