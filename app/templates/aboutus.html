About Us!

Hello this is the 'NALI Project' and our Senior Capstone for the Computer Science Academy. our project is about fast fashion and we created this website so that you can swap or giveaway clothing that you no longer want to help advocate toward fast fashion.


What is Fast Fashion?


Fast fashion is clothing that is made with cheap materials and cheap labor. It's clothing that is made to be cheap, follow the current trends, and is made to meet consumer demand. It's severely unethical and not environmentally friendly in any way.



Members!

Hi, I'm Leah Vann, a senior for the Computer Science Academy at Oakland Tech. I chose to do this topic and project because of my own personal experiences with fast fashion. I personally buy a lot of clothing but when I learned about what fast fashion was I became more aware of the clothing that I was buying and started becoming more selective with the clothing and brands I buy. After doing research I learned about different ways that you can get clothing without buying from fast fashion brands, so that is why we created this website.
